1. Information We Collect

Information You Provide to Us

Account Information:

  • Email address (for authentication)
  • Password (encrypted and never stored in plain text)
  • Profile information (name, optional)

Task and Productivity Data:

  • Tasks you create (title, description, energy level)
  • Task completion status and history
  • Focus session data (Pomodoro timer usage)
  • Task templates you create

Mood and Energy Data:

  • Your daily mood selections
  • Energy level tracking
  • Timestamps of mood/energy updates

üé§ Voice Data Protection

Your voice is processed locally and NEVER leaves your device:

  • Voice-to-text recordings are processed locally on your device
  • Voice data is never stored or transmitted to our servers
  • Transcribed text is saved as task content only if you choose to save it

Information Collected Automatically

Usage Data:

  • App features you use and how often
  • Session duration and frequency
  • Navigation patterns within the App
  • Crash reports and error logs (anonymous)

Device Information:

  • Device type and model
  • Operating system version
  • Unique device identifiers
  • App version
  • Timezone and language settings

Haptic and Notification Preferences:

  • Your haptic feedback settings
  • Notification preferences
  • ADHD-friendly feature toggles

3. Data Storage and Security

Local Storage

  • Most of your data is stored locally on your device using encrypted local database (Hive)
  • You can use the App fully offline without an internet connection
  • Voice recordings are never stored; only transcribed text is saved locally

Cloud Storage (Premium Feature)

  • Premium users can enable cloud sync to back up their data
  • Cloud data is stored using Firebase Firestore with encryption in transit and at rest
  • Cloud sync is optional and can be disabled at any time

üîê Security Measures

  • All data transmissions use HTTPS/TLS encryption
  • Passwords are hashed using industry-standard algorithms
  • Firebase Authentication with secure token management
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • No third-party access to your personal data

9. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

  • Right to Know: What personal information we collect and how we use it
  • Right to Delete: Request deletion of your personal information
  • Right to Opt-Out: Opt-out of the sale of your personal information (we don't sell data)
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: We will not discriminate against you for exercising your rights

To exercise these rights, contact us at gherman.roman@gmail.com.

10. European Privacy Rights (GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of your data
  • Right to Rectification: Correct inaccurate data
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to Data Portability: Receive your data in a portable format
  • Right to Object: Object to processing of your data
  • Right to Restriction: Restrict processing of your data
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise these rights, contact us at gherman.roman@gmail.com.

2. Description of Service

FlowPalette is a mobile application designed to help neurodivergent individuals, particularly those with ADHD, manage tasks, track mood and energy levels, and maintain focus through gentle, adaptive features.

Core Features

  • Task Management: Create, organize, and complete tasks based on your energy levels
  • Mood & Energy Tracking: Track your daily mood and energy to better plan your day
  • Focus Companion: Pomodoro timer and focus session tracking
  • Voice-to-Text: Create tasks using voice input (processed locally on your device)
  • Haptic Feedback: Gentle, ADHD-friendly haptic patterns

Premium Features

  • Unlimited task creation
  • Cloud sync across devices
  • Advanced analytics and insights
  • Gentle reminders and notifications
  • Advanced Focus Companion features
  • Full mood and energy history
  • Task templates and customization

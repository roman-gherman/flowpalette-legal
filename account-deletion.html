‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

How long does deletion take?

Your account and all associated data will be permanently deleted within 30 days of your request.

Can I recover my account after deletion?

No, account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All your data will be permanently removed from our servers.

What happens to my Premium subscription?

If you have an active Premium subscription, it will be cancelled immediately. Please note that deletion does not automatically trigger a refund. Contact us if you need a refund for an active subscription.

Will deleting the app delete my account?

No, simply uninstalling the app does not delete your account. You must explicitly delete your account using the methods described above.

What data is retained after deletion?

We retain minimal anonymous analytics data (no personal information) for legal and operational purposes. All personally identifiable data is permanently deleted.
